Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This application is designed with privacy-first principles. We collect minimal data and never sell your information to third parties.

1. Data Collection

The app collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Quit date and time
  • Number of cigarettes per day
  • Price per pack (if savings tracking is enabled)
  • Smoking triggers and reasons for quitting
  • Achievement progress
  • Relapse information (if applicable)
  • Support contact information (if provided)

All this data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData and is not transmitted to any external servers unless you explicitly enable iCloud backup (premium feature).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Track your quit smoking progress
  • Calculate health improvements and savings
  • Unlock achievements and milestones
  • Provide personalized motivation
  • Generate insights about your journey
  • Enable relapse prevention features

We do NOT use your data for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • Selling to third parties
  • Creating user profiles for external use
  • Any purpose other than app functionality

3. Data Storage

LOCAL STORAGE:

By default, all your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • Deleting the app removes all data
  • Data is protected by your device's security features

iCLOUD BACKUP (Premium):

If you enable iCloud backup:

  • Your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Only you can access this data with your Apple ID
  • Data syncs across your devices signed into the same Apple ID
  • Apple's privacy policy applies to iCloud stored data
  • You can disable iCloud backup at any time

4. Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

REVENUECAT:

  • Manages premium subscriptions
  • Only processes payment information
  • Does not have access to your health data
  • Privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

APPLE SERVICES:

  • App Store for app distribution
  • StoreKit for reviews and payments
  • iCloud for optional backup (premium only)
  • CloudKit encryption protects your data

No analytics or tracking services are used.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • All data encrypted on device using iOS security
  • No data transmission to external servers
  • iCloud data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • No user accounts or passwords required
  • No personal identifying information collected
  • Secure coding practices and regular updates

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • ACCESS: View all your data within the app
  • MODIFY: Edit your information at any time
  • DELETE: Use "Reset Data" to delete everything
  • EXPORT: Premium users can backup to iCloud
  • PORTABILITY: Your data stays with you
  • OPT-OUT: Disable any feature at any time

Since data is stored locally, you have full control without needing to contact us.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. The app is designed for adults who want to quit smoking.

If you are under 18, please consult with a parent or guardian before using this app.

8. International Users

This app is available worldwide through the App Store. Your data remains on your device regardless of your location, ensuring compliance with local privacy laws including:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA (California)
  • PIPEDA (Canada)
  • Other regional privacy regulations

Since we don't collect or transmit data to servers, we inherently comply with data localization requirements.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements.

  • Updates will be posted in the app
  • Significant changes will be highlighted
  • Continued use implies acceptance
  • Review periodically for updates

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

  • Use the App Store support page
  • Email through App Store Connect
  • Submit feedback via TestFlight (beta users)

We aim to respond within 48 hours to all privacy inquiries.
